multicombine

Perform a “linear combination in the exponent”, i.e., multiply each of the ciphers with the corresponding scalar, and sum them together. If cipher C_i ciphers is an encryption in the exponent of the value v_i, then the resulting cipher is a valid encryption in the exponent of the value \sum_i v_i \cdot s_i where s_i is the i-th scalar.

value_to_chunks

Transform a scalar into as many chunks as necessary. The chunks are returned in little-endian order.
